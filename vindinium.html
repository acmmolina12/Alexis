    What is Vindinium

    Vindinium is where students create Ai and make them fight other similar Ai's in vindinium. It's a game where you create your own Ai and make the Ai find gold and make it fight other Ai's that gets close to it. It's like minecraft because in minecraft you can also look for gold stuff and can fight other creatures that try to attack. In the vindinuim you have no control of the Ai and you program it to do specific tasks. The only difference between these two is that minecraft you don't get to fight other people, you fight animals that are dangerous and that try attacking you.
